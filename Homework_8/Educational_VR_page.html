Noctis Evadere

Educational VR project designed to raise public awareness of the strengths and weakness of robotic sensors.

Overview
Project Goals
In September 2019, I participated in a collaborative VR project with students and faculty from Oregon State University and Stuttgart Media University. This was an educational project designed to raise public awareness of the strengths and weakness of robotic sensors. We had to complete the project within a ten-week academic term.
Collaboration
We worked closely with the students from Stuttgart HdM to develop concepts that would help transofrm our educational goals into a fully-realized palyable experience. In addition to a weeklong in-person brainstorming session, we worked with these students remotely to ideate our design and share feedback. Within our own groups teams worked together to divide the work among our experitise. My main contributions were in detial modeling using Autodesk Maya and prototyping code in Unity.
